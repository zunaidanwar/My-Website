The Journey Begins

Rahi Originals was born not in a boardroom, but in a small, dust-filled workshop, from a love for materials that tell a story. Our founder, inspired by a family legacy of leather artisans, saw a world of disposable fashion and yearned for something real.

The goal was simple yet ambitious: to create timeless companions for life's journeys. Bags that weren't just about carrying things, but about carrying memories, weathering storms, and becoming more beautiful with every mile traveled.

This is not just our business, it's our heritage.

Our Philosophy

The Finest Materials

We exclusively source full-grain leather, renowned for its durability and ability to develop a rich, unique patina over time. Our hardware is solid brass, and our linings are organic, sustainable cotton.

Generational Craft

Each Rahi Original is saddle-stitched by hand, a time-honored technique far superior to machine sewing. This meticulous process is passed down through generations of artisans in our partner workshops.

Considered Design

We believe in functional minimalism. Our designs are stripped of the non-essential to focus on clean lines, practical utility, and a timeless aesthetic that complements any journey, for any generation.

The Hands Behind the Craft

We partner with a small, family-run workshop where leatherwork is not just a job, but a revered art form. By choosing Rahi, you are not only acquiring a piece of lasting quality, but also supporting these master artisans and helping to preserve their invaluable heritage.
